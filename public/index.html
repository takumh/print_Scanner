Here are a few tips and suggestions on how to get the most out of this service:


  • Make sure you are scanning printed text, handwriting text will not be recognized
  • Text on a white background is best scanned compared to other colours
  • Avoid having background noise (anything that is not the text or paper) in the image you take
  • Make sure your camera is clean and clear before you begin scanning
